Information We Collect

We may collect personal and business information that you provide to us directly when you:

  • Create an account or user profile.

  • Make payments or complete transactions.

 

The information we may collect includes, but is not limited to:

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email address, phone number).

  • Transaction data (e.g., purchase history, sales records).

 

2. Information We Automatically Collect

When you use our services, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information (e.g., IP address, browser type, operating system).
  • Usage information (e.g., pages visited, actions taken).
  • Location data (if you enable location services).

We may use your information for the following purposes:

  1. Providing Services: To deliver, maintain, and improve our POS software and related services.
