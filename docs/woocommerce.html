Omnichannel Retail Solution

Integrate your WooCommerce store with Slimrate POS for true omnichannel retail. Manage inventory, orders, and customers across online and in-store channels from one unified platform.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Inventory Synchronization - Automatically sync inventory levels between your online store and physical locations. Prevent overselling and stockouts.
  • Unified Order Management - View and manage all orders from one dashboard. Process online orders at any POS terminal.
  • Product Information Sync - Update product details, prices, and images in one place. Changes instantly reflect across all channels.
  • Multi-Channel Reporting - Comprehensive analytics across online and in-store sales. Track performance by channel, product, and location.
  • Customer Data Integration - Unified customer profiles across channels. Reward online and in-store purchases with one loyalty program.
  • Buy Online, Pick Up In Store (BOPIS) - Enable click-and-collect functionality. Fulfill online orders from any store location.
  • Online Returns In-Store - Process online order returns at POS terminals. Provide flexible return options for customers.
  • Loyalty Program Integration - Customers earn and redeem rewards both online and in-store. Single loyalty account across all channels.
