Advanced Security Integration

Connect your Hikvision security cameras with Slimrate POS to create a powerful security and loss prevention system. Every transaction is automatically linked to video footage.

Key Features

  • Transaction-Video Synchronization - Every POS transaction is automatically linked to corresponding video footage. Review suspicious transactions with one click.
  • Multi-Camera Support - Monitor multiple camera angles simultaneously. View different perspectives of the same transaction.
  • Cloud Video Storage - Secure cloud storage for all video footage. Access recordings from anywhere with internet connection.
  • Remote Monitoring Access - View live camera feeds remotely from any device. Check in on your business anytime, anywhere.
  • Advanced Analytics and Alerts - Receive alerts for unusual activities. AI-powered analytics detect suspicious behaviors.
  • Exception Reporting - Automatically flag transactions with voids, refunds, or discounts for video review.
  • Employee Activity Monitoring - Track employee activities at the register. Reduce internal theft and improve accountability.
  • Incident Investigation - Quickly search and export video clips for specific transactions or time periods.
