What is Homebase?

Homebase is a comprehensive workforce management solution that helps businesses manage their teams more effectively. When integrated with Slimrate POS, it creates a powerful ecosystem for managing both your sales operations and your workforce from a single platform.

Key Features

  • Automated Employee Scheduling - Create and manage employee schedules with drag-and-drop simplicity. Automatically notify staff of schedule changes.
  • Real-Time Time Clock Tracking - Employees clock in and out directly from the POS terminal. Accurate time tracking eliminates manual timesheets.
  • Seamless Payroll Integration - Export time and attendance data directly to your payroll system. Reduce errors and save hours of manual data entry.
  • Team Communication Tools - Built-in messaging keeps your team connected. Share updates, announcements, and important information instantly.
  • Labor Cost Management - Monitor labor costs in real-time. Get insights into labor percentage and optimize scheduling for profitability.
  • Shift Swapping - Employees can request shift swaps directly through the app, subject to manager approval.
  • Time-Off Requests - Manage vacation and sick leave requests digitally. Track PTO balances automatically.
  • Compliance Tools - Stay compliant with labor laws including break requirements, overtime rules, and minor work restrictions.

Benefits of Integration

Eliminate double data entry and manual timesheet calculations. Automatically sync employee hours between Homebase and your POS system.

Optimize scheduling based on sales forecasts and historical data. Prevent overstaffing and reduce unnecessary overtime expenses.
