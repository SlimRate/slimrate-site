What is the difference between online and offline transactions?

Online debit transactions call for customers to endorse payments by submitting their personal identification number at the point of sale. While offline transactions require shoppers to sign sales receipt.

Can I accept contactless payments?

Yes. Slimrate supports contactless and EMV payments.

What is an offline payment?

An offline payment is a transaction which does not require internet or telecom connectivity.
