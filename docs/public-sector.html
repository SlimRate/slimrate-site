A Modern Commerce Platform for Today's Public Agencies

Slimrate delivers a fully integrated platform for point-of-sale (POS), payments, e-commerce, reporting, and constituent-facing transactions. Our solutions help government agencies, schools, and nonprofits streamline how they collect fees, manage revenue, optimize workflows, and serve their communities.

Slimrate provides secure, scalable, cloud-based technology that adapts to the complexity of public-sector operations—whether you manage a city utility department, a county parks system, a university campus, or a large public venue.
